About coffee pod

Coffeepod was founded in the Summer of 2020. Our goal was to make a platform in which students could connect with potential mentors and mentees. We envisioned a place wher people would feel encouraged to reach out to others to ask for guidance. When we thought of coffeepod, the platforms that existed relied either on matching mentors and mentees, which limits the number of people mentees and mentors can interact with, or presented an overwhelming among of information that may deter users from joining. We wanted to develop a simple and accessible website where people seeking guidance could easily make new connections whether they want to give or receive advice.
